What the Club is About

We are a student let club organization that believes in helping Computer Science students succeed in the STEM field. We offer events that are aimed and designed to grow your understanding of tech related skills.

Whether you're aiming to be a Cybersecurity Analyst, Software Engineer, Data Scientist, UI UX Designer, or Project Manager, we offer a community of like-minded individuals who are eager to connect and learn with others.

Events

Interested in learning about Cybersecurity, Capture the Flag, Ethical Hacking, React, Python, CSS/HTML/JS, or Swift? Then we are the club for you! We offer workshops for each of these programming languages, topics, and more, including Networking and Technical interview prep workshops. Whether you are a complete beginner or an advanced programmer/Cybersecurity analyst, come join us at any of our events.

Resources

Are you not sure where to start or where to look for resources? We compiled a bunch of resources that we believe are best suited to help you out in this endeavor. These resources include information on Computer Science classes at John Jay, Cybersecurity, Ethical Hacking, Data Science, Web Development, getting started on a new language, and much more.
