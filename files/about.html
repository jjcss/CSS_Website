Who We Are

The Computer Science Society (CSS) Club is dedicated to promoting the STEM field, specifically computer science, technology, and mathematics, because changing the world for the better all begins with how to utilize technology. We work to provide resources for any student, alumni, and professionals regardless of an individual's degree of knowledge or commitment in the field of computer science. CSS meets and hosts events that provide opportunities for networking, educational, and professional development related to computer science and technology. Our mission is to provide students with the skills, resources, motivation, and training necessary to become successful leaders in STEM.

We are a community of like-minded individuals who are eager to network and succeed. We aim to help every individual grow as a person and as a tech professional to feel confident in their tech skills once they graduate. We treasure uniqueness in each and every individual. We encourage everyone to share their love for technology and to uplift each other throughout their entire college years and beyond. The club shines when everyone shines. Are you excited? If so, come join us at the CSS Club in CUNY John Jay!

Conquer LeetCode

We know that in most sectors in the tech field, you will have to go on the “LeetCode Grind.” Hence, we have LeetCode challenge contests so you can build up the necessary Data Structure skills to complete more LeetCode problems and ace your technical interview. We also have a LeetCode bot in our discord to help you out.

Discussion With Your Peers

Join our club’s Discord server to collaborate, chat, have a good time with your peers, and stay up to date with anything club related. Be on the lookout for any cool club giveaways.
