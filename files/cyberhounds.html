What is CTF?

Abbreviated for “Capture The Flag”, CTF involves various challenges, ranging topics from Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) to Web Application Exploitation, Cryptography, Enamuration & Exploitation.

Flag Captured !

How It Works

Meetings are hybrid, though it is encouraged to join in person as this will help you network with others. To be placed on rosters. We participate in various leagues such as Cyber Skyline NCL, AngstromCTF, and San Diego CTF.

Sufficient participation or willingness to compete must be demonstrated in some way.
